Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is a vibrant and bustling metropolis located on the southeastern coast of India. As the capital of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu, Chennai is a cultural, economic, and educational hub of South India. It boasts a rich history, dating back to ancient times when it served as a prominent trading port for the British East India Company.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, often referred to as the "City of Pearls," is a vibrant and historic metropolis located in the southern part of India. It is the capital of the Indian state of Telangana and shares its identity as a major technology and business hub with the neighboring state of Andhra Pradesh. Hyderabad is renowned for its rich history, culture, and cuisine, making it a unique and captivating destination.

Rajkot is a vibrant city located in the western Indian state of Gujarat. It serves as the administrative headquarters of the Rajkot District and is known for its rich cultural heritage, thriving industry, and historical significance.
